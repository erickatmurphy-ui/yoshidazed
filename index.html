Guest Weekend Guide

Welcome to YOSHIDAZED

A Scottsdale weekend for Nicole and Brandon.

You are not here by accident. You are here because someone decided you were emotionally, socially, or logistically necessary to the plot.

Over the next few days, Production HQ may text you, assign you tasks, question your loyalty, reward suspicious behavior, and ask you to explain yourself on camera.
