Quick Check

Day-Of Reminders

Before we get too far into the weekend, make sure you have:

  • ID
  • Phone charger
  • Comfortable shoes for ATV
  • Swimsuit accessible
  • Saturday night outfit
  • Any medications you need
  • Sunglasses or hat
  • Anything you personally need that nobody else can replace

For the drive

  • Keep snacks/drinks within reach
  • Save your phone battery
  • Drivers are off-limits for any drinking games
  • Production-related texts are for passengers only while the car is moving

Everything else

Everything else is handled.

Public Rules

Keep It Fun

Weekend Energy

Participate when you want, rest when you need, and keep the focus on celebrating Nicole and Brandon.

Game Etiquette

Read your printed cards, follow public prompts, and keep playful suspicion kind. No one has to explain anything they do not want to share.

Alcohol

Drinking is optional. Water, mocktails, dares, or skipping a prompt are always acceptable.

Safety

Drivers never drink. Do not film, text, or complete prompts while driving or riding ATVs.
